# Ethics in Cybersecurity

Ethics in cybersecurity refers to the responsible and lawful use of security knowledge and skills.

## Core Principles
- Always obtain authorization before testing systems.
- Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information.
- Do not damage or alter systems maliciously.
- Follow legal and professional standards.

## Personal Reflection
Ethical responsibility is essential in cybersecurity because technical skills must always be guided by integrity and legality.
