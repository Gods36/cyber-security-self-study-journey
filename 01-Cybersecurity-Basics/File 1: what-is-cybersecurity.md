# What is Cybersecurity?

## Definition
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, applications, and data from cyber threats, unauthorized access, damage, or theft.

## Importance of Cybersecurity
- Protects sensitive and personal information
- Prevents financial loss and fraud
- Maintains privacy and confidentiality
- Ensures systems remain available and functional
- Builds trust in digital systems

## Personal Reflection
Cybersecurity is not just about hacking; it is about protecting systems responsibly and ensuring digital safety.
