# What is Cybersecurity?

## Definition
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting devices, networks, and data from cyber threats.

## Importance of Cybersecurity
- Protects personal information: It keeps your passwords, bank details, private messages from hackers.
- Prevents financial loss: It stops online fruad, scams, and theft of money from bank account.
- Ensure privacy: It helps keep your personal and confidential datas from being leaked or misused.
- Supports businesses and governments: Companies and governments rely on cybersecurity to protect their sensitive data and keep their services running smoothly.
- Protects devices: It protects devices from cyber threats that can cause damage to devices.
- Builds trust in digital systems: When systems are secure, people feel safe using them.

## Personal Reflection
Cybersecurity is not just about hacking; it is about protecting systems responsibly and ensuring digital safety.
